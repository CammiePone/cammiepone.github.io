Cammie's Wearable Backpacks


09 Feb 2022

This will be a short log, but I was made aware of a funny bug in my backpack mod today.

Apparently if someone else wore a pride pack, everyone else's backpack's texture would swap out to that of the pride pack's (as seen below).



Of course, as much as I find it funny and enjoy it, it is a bug and as a result, needs to be fixed. It was due to the texture variable being static, and therefore was applied effectively universally to every player.

As I said, this'll be a short one, as it's now fixed and backpacks look like they should.
